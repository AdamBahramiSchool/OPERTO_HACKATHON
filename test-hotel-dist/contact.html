Getting Here

Test Hotel is located on the iconic Ocean Drive in the heart of Miami Beach's Art Deco Historic District.

  • From Miami International Airport: Take I-195 East to Miami Beach. Follow Collins Avenue south to 12th Street, turn right to Ocean Drive. Approximately 25 minutes.
  • By Bus: Miami-Dade Transit Route 119 stops one block from the hotel on Collins Avenue.
  • Parking: Valet parking available at $35/night. Public garage at 12th Street and Collins Avenue.
