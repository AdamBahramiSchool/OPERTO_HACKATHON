About Test Hotel

Founded in 1998 by the Alvarez family, Test Hotel has been welcoming guests to Miami Beach for over 25 years. What started as a 12-room Art Deco guesthouse on Ocean Drive has grown into a 120-room boutique luxury hotel — yet we've never lost the personal touch that makes us different from the big chains.

Our team is made up of Miami Beach locals who love this city as much as our guests do. From recommending the best ceviche in Little Havana to securing reservations at Komodo for date night, we go beyond check-in and check-out.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic South Florida hospitality — warm, unhurried, and genuinely personal — in one of the most iconic locations in the world.

Community & Sustainability

Test Hotel partners with the Miami Beach Botanical Garden and the Surfrider Foundation to protect Miami Beach's natural environment. We source 80% of our restaurant ingredients from Florida-based farms and seafood suppliers.
