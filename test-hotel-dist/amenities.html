Hotel Amenities

At Test Hotel in Miami Beach, your experience extends well beyond your room. Our facilities are designed to complement the best of South Florida living.

Rooftop Infinity Pool

Our heated rooftop infinity pool overlooks the Atlantic Ocean and Miami Beach skyline. Open daily 7 AM – 10 PM. Poolside cocktail service available from our Horizon Bar. Towels, lounge chairs, and cabanas provided. Cabana reservations recommended for weekends.

The Drift Spa

A full-service spa offering signature South Florida treatments inspired by the Caribbean and Latin American traditions. Services include:

  • Swedish and deep tissue massage
  • Cuban coffee body scrub
  • Seaweed and mineral wrap
  • Couples treatment rooms
  • Sauna and steam rooms
  • Facial treatments using locally sourced aloe and coconut oil

Open daily 9 AM – 8 PM. Reservations required: (305) 555-0199

Horizon — Rooftop Restaurant & Bar

Chef Derek Huang's award-winning rooftop restaurant serves New American cuisine with Cuban and Caribbean influences. Seasonal menus featuring Florida stone crab (in season), Florida Keys yellowtail snapper, and tropical fruit desserts.

Breakfast: 7 AM – 11 AM | Lunch: 12 PM – 3 PM | Dinner: 6 PM – 11 PM

Horizon Bar open until midnight Friday and Saturday.

Oceanside Fitness Center

State-of-the-art fitness center with Peloton bikes, Technogym cardio machines, free weights, and TRX suspension training. Personal training sessions available. Open 24 hours for hotel guests.

Beach Club

Test Hotel guests enjoy reserved beach access on Miami Beach with complimentary lounge chairs and umbrellas. Our beach butlers provide towel service, snack menus, and watersport equipment rentals including paddleboards and snorkeling gear.

Concierge Services

Our Miami Beach concierge team can arrange:

  • Restaurant reservations across Miami and Miami Beach
  • Private yacht and boat charters departing from Miami Beach Marina
  • Everglades and Biscayne Bay eco-tours
  • Tickets to Art Basel Miami Beach (December) and Ultra Music Festival
  • Private shopping tours at Bal Harbour Shops and Design District
  • Airport transfers and car service
